ABOUT

Often I'm getting dirty with my hands and trying to bringing back of the napkin designs to reality. My interests prompted me to pursue a degree in Chemistry to further my life-time goal of becoming an opthamologis. I focused my entire undergraduate degree on applying the concepts of what I was taught to fundamental principles around the developtment and detection of defects within the eye.

As time went on, I was able to digest my own ideas and realized that I truely love learning everything that has an edge of mystery to it and decided to take the mcat and apply for medschool in Washington.

I strive to channel my energy and drive too helping others and finding the joys that the world has to offer. I pride myself on making others feel welcome and loved, so that growth is without loss, and life-long goals are reached.
